Matriculation Hub
A mini-site for enrolled Fellows and families to find everything they need once they're in — schedule, transportation, orientation info. Separate from Forrest's applicant system.
Schedule Look-Up
Fellows and families can look up their assigned program night, campus, and cohort. Should auto-update from the scheduling source of truth once Chasity finalizes assignments.
Transportation Routes
Published routes with pickup/dropoff times and locations. Marcus configures; Fellows and families can reference on their own without having to ask staff.
Orientation Notes & Info
Date, location, what to bring, what to expect. Segmented for new vs. returning Fellows where needed. Families who miss orientation can find what they need here.
Forms & Next Steps
Intake and matriculation forms for new Fellows. Easy reference for families who missed something or need to resubmit.
Note on Forrest's existing system
Forrest's current site (thebryc.github.io/newfellows/) is for applicants to look up their name and check their status. That's a different function from this hub. We could potentially add these HTML pages to his site, but that's a separate decision — the two shouldn't be conflated in planning.
The app is internal-facing only. This hub and BRYCdown are both external-facing — schedule look-up, transportation routes, orientation info, and forms should live here and connect to BRYCdown so families have one consistent place to land regardless of how they get there. BRYCdown is Google-based and easily editable, so the two can stay in sync without hard-coding anything.
